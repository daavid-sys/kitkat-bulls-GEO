# Product Concept: SWARM

## One-liner
Find every conversation on the internet where your brand should show up — obvious and non-obvious — and help your team engage in your brand's authentic voice.

## Thesis
"Go where the attention is." Content and search are increasingly filtered by LLMs and algorithms. A 3-person marketing team can't compete with HubSpot's army — but an AI tool can make those 3 people omnipresent, like Duolingo's social team powered by AI.

## Core Loop
1. **Peec AI** → which topics you're invisible on in AI search (strategic intelligence)
2. **Tavily** → find live conversations on LinkedIn, Reddit, X about those topics + adjacent/cultural ones
3. **Gemini** → score relevance (including non-obvious connections) + learn brand voice
4. **Team** → sees opportunity feed → picks conversations → gets voice-aware draft → engages

## The Flywheel
Peec detects invisibility → SWARM finds conversations → Team engages → More brand mentions → AI engines learn from them → Peec shows improved visibility → repeat

## What Makes It Different
- **Not monitoring** — offensive. Finding conversations to JOIN, not mentions to track.
- **Non-obvious connections** — like Duolingo commenting on breakup posts. AI scores adjacent and cultural relevance.
- **Brand voice engine** — learns your voice from website + social posts, drafts sound like YOU, not ChatGPT.
- **Peec AI as strategic brain** — doesn't just find any conversation, finds the ones that matter for AI visibility.

## Three Connection Types
| Type | What it means | Example for Attio |
|------|--------------|-------------------|
| DIRECT | Obviously about your category | "What CRM are you using?" |
| ADJACENT | Related problem/workflow | "My startup hit 50 people and everything is chaos" |
| CULTURAL | Non-obvious, creative brand angle | Meme: "POV: copy-pasting contacts between 4 apps" |

## Partner Tech
1. Google DeepMind (Gemini) — AI backbone
2. Tavily — discovery engine (web search across platforms)
3. Entire — agent traceability

## Tech Stack
- React + Vite (hand-coded)
- No backend — all client-side API calls
- Peec AI REST API + Tavily API + Gemini API

## Name
Working title: **SWARM** — "3 marketers, swarming every conversation."
